What is General practice?

General practicioners, or GP for short, are the primary care contact.This means that they are the first type of people we contact unless it is an emergency.
This can include:

  • assessing and diagnosing a patient
  • Being able to find the right treatment for a patient
  • referring patients to secondary care/hospital
  • Being able to work in clinics, hospitals and if needed, care homes and homes
  • Science skills
  • Knowledge of psychology
  • to be able to use a computer and the main software packages competently
